# COOKIE POLICY

**LAST UPDATED:** December 2, 2025

## 1. INTRODUCTION
Our website, **[SWENAUTOS.COM]**, uses cookies to improve your experience. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices.

## 2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and provide a better experience.

## 3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE
- **Essential Cookies:** Necessary for our website to function (e.g., login, payment processing)
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how you use our website (e.g., Google Analytics)
- **Marketing Cookies:** Used for advertising and tracking (e.g., Facebook Pixel)
- **Functional Cookies:** Enhance website functionality (e.g., language preferences)

## 4. HOW WE USE COOKIES
We use cookies to:
- Authenticate and personalize your experience
- Analyze website usage and improve performance
- Display targeted advertising
- Store preferences and settings

## 5. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES
We work with third-party service providers who use cookies on our website, including:
- Google Analytics
- Facebook
- Payment gateways

## 6. YOUR CHOICES
You can:
- Accept or decline cookies through your browser settings
- Delete cookies at any time
- Opt-out of certain cookies using our cookie management tool

## 7. COOKIE MANAGEMENT
You can manage cookies through your browser settings or our cookie management tool, available on our website.

## 8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
