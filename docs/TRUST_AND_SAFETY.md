# TRUST AND SAFETY

## Your Security, Our Priority at SWENAUTOS

At **[SWENAUTOS]**, we take the security and trust of our customers very seriously. We're committed to providing a safe and secure online shopping experience, so you can shop with confidence.

## How We Protect You

- **Secure Payment Processing:** We use industry-leading encryption and security protocols to protect your payment information
- **Verified Sellers:** Our team verifies sellers to ensure authenticity and trustworthiness
- **Buyer Protection:** We're committed to helping you get a refund or replacement if your order doesn't arrive or isn't as described
- **24/7 Monitoring:** Our team monitors our platform around the clock to detect and prevent suspicious activity

## Your Role in Staying Safe

- **Use Strong Passwords:** Choose a unique and strong password for your account
- **Keep Your Account Info Up-to-Date:** Make sure your account information is accurate and current
- **Be Cautious of Phishing Scams:** Be wary of emails or messages that ask for your personal or financial information

## Our Safety Measures

- **TLS Encryption:** We use TLS encryption to protect your data in transit
- **Regular Security Audits:** We conduct regular security audits to ensure our platform remains secure
- **Compliance with Industry Standards:** We comply with industry standards for payment security, including PCI-DSS
