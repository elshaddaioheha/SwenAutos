# DISPUTE RESOLUTION

## Resolving Issues Quickly and Fairly

At **[SWENAUTOS]**, we're committed to providing a fair and transparent dispute resolution process for all customers. If you have a concern or issue with your order, please follow these steps:

## STEP 1: CONTACT US

Contact our customer support team as soon as possible to report your issue. We'll do our best to resolve it promptly.

- **Email:** [SWENAUTOS@GMAIL.COM]
- **Phone:** [PHONE NUMBER]
- **Chat:** Live chat available on our website

## STEP 2: PROVIDE INFORMATION

Please provide the following information to help us investigate and resolve your issue:
- Order number
- Description of the issue
- Any relevant documentation (e.g., receipts, photos, communication records)

## STEP 3: INVESTIGATION AND RESOLUTION

We'll investigate your issue and respond with a resolution within **5 business days**. Our resolution may include:
- Refund or replacement of the product
- Cancellation of the order
- Other reasonable solution

## STEP 4: ESCALATION

If you're not satisfied with our resolution, you can escalate the issue to our internal complaints officer:
- **Email:** [SWENAUTOS@GMAIL.COM]
- **Phone:** [PHONE NUMBER]

## DISPUTE RESOLUTION PROCESS

- We'll acknowledge receipt of your complaint within **24 hours**
- We'll investigate and respond with a resolution within **5 business days**
- If we need more time, we'll inform you and provide an updated timeline
- You may pursue external remedies through applicable regulatory authorities if needed

## Escalation to Regulatory Authorities

If you believe we have not resolved your dispute fairly, you may escalate to:
- **Nigeria Data Protection Commissioner** (for data-related issues)
- **Relevant Consumer Protection Agency** (for product/service disputes)

*This Dispute Resolution process is designed to provide fair and transparent handling of customer concerns in accordance with Nigerian consumer protection laws.*
